<h1>Welcome to the Cross Government Software Engineering Community</h1> <h2>Who are we</h2> <p>We are a group of engineers working in software engineering across government, some of us are hands on developers, others working in other technical or management roles.</p> <h2>Vision</h2> <p>To provide a forum for collaboration for software engineers across government</p> <ul> <li>To learn and to teach</li> <li>To share ideas</li> <li>To have some fun</li> </ul> <h2>How are we doing this</h2> <ul> <li>Running monthly virtual lean coffee sessions</li> <li>Developing our GitHub repo</li> <li>Looking to run more themed events</li> <li>Hosting a virtual software engineering meetup in 2022</li> <li>We have a small facilitation group that is doing some of the initial setup</li> </ul> <h2>Do you want to help facilitate the group ?</h2> <p>Drop an email to <a href="mailto:Andy.Poole@ukho.gov.uk">Andy Poole</a> or <a href="mailto:david.heath@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk">David Heath</a> and one of us can add you to the facilitators group</p> <h2>We want you to be part of this - why not get involved?</h2> <ul> <li>Signup to our <a href="https://uk-cross-government-software-engineering-community.mailchimpsites.com/">email mailing list on mail chimp</a> - You'll need to signup with your gov email address - just sign up below. You will get a welcome email and will then be added onto our mailing list of events going forward.</li> <li>Check out our <a href="https://github.com/uk-x-gov-software-community/">GitHub Repo</a></li> <li>Existing members check out our <a href="https://github.com/uk-x-gov-software-community/community-space/discussions">Discussions</a> (must be a member of our github organisation)</li> </ul> <h2>How we use GitHub</h2> <p>We use github to manage our group - once you sign up to our mailing list you will (assuming you have given a GitHub id) be auto enrolled on our organisation. You can then see meeting notes and join discussions on the discussion link.</p> <p>Pull requests are welcomed for this site see the readme on our <a href="https://github.com/uk-x-gov-software-community/">GitHub Repo</a> for more details.</p> <hr /> <br /> <a href="https://uk-cross-government-software-engineering-community.mailchimpsites.com/" role="button" draggable="false" class="govuk-button govuk-button--start" data-module="govuk-button"> Join Mailing List <svg class="govuk-button__start-icon" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" width="17.5" height="19" viewBox="0 0 33 40" aria-hidden="true" focusable="false"> <path fill="currentColor" d="M0 0h13l20 20-20 20H0l20-20z" /> </svg> </a>
